Why Tennet?

TenneT is the only network that was built to protect your privacy. We rely on three principles to ensure that TenneT is the best network for renters.

Personalization

TenneT was built by tenants for tenants. To ensure you have the best experience possible, there are extensive customization options for privacy and beyond!

Privacy and Security

TenneT is the only network that uses end-to-end encryption as well as Contextual Integrity to ensure that your interactions are kept private from anyone you might want to keep out: whether inside or outside of our network.

Community Moderation

Content moderation is performed both algorithmically and through a set of thoroughly vetted community tenant leaders, to ensure that your speech is always respected.
